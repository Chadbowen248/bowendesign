About Us

We love what we do. We are Chad and Meghana Bowen. A husband and wife team and self taught web developers. We both come from design backgrounds, which gave us an edge in the world of technology. Even before we got into development, we were intrigued by the inner workings of the web. All this time spent online, got us curious enough to take a good look. That’s all it took. A few small projects and websites later, we were hooked.

Meghana, is cautious and organized, while Chad is more laid-back and kind of obsessive about getting things exactly right. We balance each other out pretty well, and ultimately end up with great work. Our goal is to make functional, fast and beautiful websites and applications. Life has been amazing so far and we can’t wait to see what’s next.
